To close the handbook at the enterprise level. This chapter shows that governance is not separate from architecture; it becomes useful when it is attached to real control points, real operating processes, and shared infrastructure. That lets you map the handbook to Orcaworks’ long-term value proposition without ever explicitly selling it.
