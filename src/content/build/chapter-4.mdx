## Context as a Controlled Input Layer

- What “context” really means in enterprise AI: user input, retrieval, memory, tool outputs
- Why unsafe context is the first systems problem, not just a prompt problem

**Why we are writing the chapter**

To solve the first major problem from Part I. This chapter explains that safe AI starts with controlling what the system can see. It also maps naturally to the kind of value Orcaworks provides around context engineering and governed context flow, without turning the chapter into a product pitch.
