## From Prototype to Production Systems

- What changes when AI moves from demo workflows to live business processes
- The shift from isolated assistants to shared infrastructure and reusable control layers
- Why enterprise deployment needs common services for context, identity, policy, and execution
- The role of reference architecture in reducing fragmentation and hidden risk
- What technical leaders should expect from a production-grade AI platform

**Why we are writing the chapter**

To show that solving the problem once at prototype level is not enough. This chapter reframes deployment as an architectural step-change, and creates a natural path to discussing shared infrastructure and reference architecture in an Orcaworks-aligned but non-salesy way.
